Dr. Yonggang Qi

Lecturer

(Assistant Professor)

Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China
Email

qiyg at bupt.edu.cn

I am a Leturer at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I was a member of the Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems(PRIS) group at BUPT , as well as the SketchX lab at Queen Mary University of London. Supported by Aalborg University, I was visiting Aalborg University as a guest PhD student in 2013.

My research interests include object recognition, contour grouping, image understanding and vision-based bigdata.
