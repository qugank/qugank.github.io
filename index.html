About me

I’m Yonggang Qi, currently a Leturer at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. I recieved Ph.D degree in signal processing from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2015. My research interests include object recognition, contour grouping, image processing, computer vision and machine learning. Supported by Aalborg University, I was visiting Aalborg University as a guest PhD student in 2013.
